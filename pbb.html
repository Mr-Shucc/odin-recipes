How to make a peanut butter banana sandwich with honey

Discription

The same great classic sandwich we all know and love, but with a little added sweetness.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (pick your favorite)
  • jar of your favorite peanut butter
  • 1 large banana
  • bottle of honey (local sourced honey is best)

Directions

  1. Place your slices of bread on a flat surface beside each other
  2. With a utensil of your choosing, spread peanut butter onto one side of both pieces of bread
  3. Cut bananna into slices and evenly disperse them onto one of the peanut butter covered bread slices
  4. drizzle honey over top of the slice of bread with the bananas
  5. complete the sandwich by placing the second, banana and honeyless, bread slice, peanut butter side down, onto the other slice of bread
  6. Enjoy with great satisfaction!
