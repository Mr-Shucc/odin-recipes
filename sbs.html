Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Discription:

A creamy strawberry banana smoothie is always a favorite of mine. Not only is it delightfuly tasty, but it is also filled with nutrient needed for a healthy meal replacment.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 chopped banana
  • 3/4 cup strawberries
  • 3 ice cubes
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon ground flax seed
  • 1 teaspoon ground chia seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Blend almond milk, banana, strawberries, ice cubes, protein powder, vanilla extract, honey, ground flax seeds, ground chia seeds, and cinnamon in a blender until smooth.
